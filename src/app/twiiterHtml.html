Many of you have asked what am I reading now in regards to #HBBOOKSFROMBED. I'm reading Stillness Speaks by Eckhart Tolle. He's one of my favorite spiritual authors and I have read all of of his works starting with The Power Of Now many years ago. This past year I've faced many challenges. This book always reminds me that I must go inward, be quiet and still in order to find my inner flashlight. I have learned that in times of struggle and confusion stillness is the first step to unraveling the drama. Turning into silence requires unlearning and deconstructing lifetimes of learned patterns which is not always easy. Stillness Speaks has inspired my state of being through the enlightenment of quiet meditation that has proven to be quite transformative. It's an easy slow burn read that packs considerable wisdom that suggests that there is a deeper "I" that has nothing to do with past or future, but everything to do with the here and ever precious present...the NOW!
